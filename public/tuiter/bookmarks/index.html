For all of you heading back to school soon I just wanted to remind you all about what hypothesis are! An observational hypothesis--> is a statement that provides a descriptive explanation for previous observations - these allow you to make predictions about future observations. A working observation is a prediction about the outcomes of specific experiments. These are based on working hypothesis and details exactly what you are doing.
